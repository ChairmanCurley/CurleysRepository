CAT STUFF

Cats are curious, playful, and sometimes a little mischievous. They love to nap in sunny spots and chase anything that moves.

Also they are gloriously fluffy, which is always a bonus in my book!

Some of my favourite things about cats are as follows, although I'm sure that you'll have your own ideas on the best things about cats. I like it when cats are asleep, and it looks like they're dreaming because they start twitching and making noises - so cute. Something else I absolutely love is how much they like to snuggle and be close to you.

If you're ever feeling a bit low....just grab a cat and you'll be as right as rain in no-time flat.

Things Cats Like

  • Sleeping
  • Chasing toys
  • Climbing furniture
  • Snuggling
